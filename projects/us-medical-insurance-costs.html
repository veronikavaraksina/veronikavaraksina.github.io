1. What are the average insurance charges in each region?¶

In [6]:
ins_region = ins.groupby(['region']).charges.mean().reset_index()
pivoted_ins_region = ins_region.pivot(columns = 'region', values = 'charges')

sns.set_theme(palette="pastel")
ax = sns.barplot(x = 'region', y = 'charges', data = ins_region, hue='region')
plt.legend(bbox_to_anchor = (1.05, 1), loc = 2, borderaxespad = 0.)
plt.title('Insurance charges by region')
ax.set_xticklabels(['North East', 'North West', 'South East', 'South West'])
ax.set_xlabel('Regions')
ax.set_ylabel('Insurance Costs')
plt.show()

As we can see the highest insurance charges are in South East region, whereas the lowest charges are in South West region. The average prices in all region do not fall below 12000 USD.
